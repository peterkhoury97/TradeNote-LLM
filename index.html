TradeNote LLM User Guide

🔑 1. Set Up Your Gemini API Key

Enter your Gemini API key in the top bar and click "Save". Your key is kept secure and used only locally in your browser session.

🤖 2. Model Selector Option

If you encounter key authorization or service limits, use the dropdown next to your API Key to switch between standard models like gemini-2.5-flash and gemini-1.5-flash.

📁 3. Upload Your Financial Charts

Drag & drop stock charts, crypto charts, candlestick images, or PDF research papers directly into the left side dropzone.

✍️ 4. Use the Interactive Drawing Layer

Toggle the Draw tool on the center screen! Trace patterns, support lines, flag structures, or breakout arrows directly. When you ask a question, the LLM will see your draw markups layered over the base chart!

💬 5. Consult the AI Trading Desk

Ask tailored questions or choose one of our quick preset prompts (e.g., "Should I buy or sell?"). The LLM evaluates your drawing layout and provides clear ratings, target zones, and probability analysis.
